## 💡 Tips for a Strong Resume Entry

- **Lead with a verb:** Analyzed, Built, Designed, Identified, Delivered.
- **Quantify:** rows analyzed, % margin, # of recommendations, $ of sales.
- **Show the "so what":** don't just say you built a chart — say what it revealed
  or enabled.
- **Match the job post:** if a posting says "Power BI," mention that your Tableau
  skills transfer, or rebuild the dashboard in Power BI later.
